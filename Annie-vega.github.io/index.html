About Me

Getting to know the person behind the projects

It's a hard process to try to introduce yourself away from your job, what you studied, where you live, or your love life, but I'd like to share some details about me.

I get easily excited about things around me - I love to notice the beauties of the world around us and take attentive note of the things I'm constantly grateful for. I'm also aware of my privileges and constantly looking for the best possible way to help and give back.

One of my biggest motivations in life is to help and give back - that's why I'm committed to helping as much as possible, including making some hard decisions such as the ones I discuss here. This path of looking for ways to help has been my ultimate "life guide" and the experience has been incredibly rewarding and emotionally challenging.

I try to be present and conscious about my surroundings - all this has ended up with more questions than I could answer. Hopefully, this website can help with some answers and generate even more questions.
